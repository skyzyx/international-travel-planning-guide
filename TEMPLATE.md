### üïì Understanding Time Zones

**UTC:** Coordinated Universal Time; formerly **GMT** = Greenwich Mean Time.

* Every time zone on earth is either positive or negative time from UTC.
* UTC never has daylight savings. Daylight savings changes the offsets from UTC.
* The line goes from North ‚Üí South through London, England, UK.
* The opposite line on the other side of the world goes from North ‚Üí South and is called the International Date Line. It runs though the Pacific Ocean.

### ‚ùå Scams to Avoid!

1. [Big City] _Assume_ that people are trying to scam you.
2. If someone tries to start trouble, offer to let the police sort-out the situation, and look for an officer.

[Is there a YouTube video that explains things well for LOCATION?]

* üö´ Once you leave the airport, NEVER EVER give anyone your Passport/ID. Not even if they are dressed like an officer/official.
* üö´ Someone tries to push a CD into your hand.
* üö´ Someone tries to put a bracelet on your arm (e.g., fake monk, visitor from another country).
* üö´ Someone asks for donations (e.g., disaster relief, orphans, something else that makes you sad).
* üö´ Someone asks for money to take a bus/train. Especially if it's a specific amount of money.
* üö´ Someone bumps into you, drops something, then wants you to pay for it.
* üö´ Guessing games on the street ‚Äî Find the ball, pick the card, etc.
* üö´ Bird Poop Scam ‚Äî You think a bird pooped on you, but someone comes up to help clean you off. (Pick-pockets.)
* üö´ Ask for prices of things up-front. Some businesses will overcharge you because you are a tourist, or will bring you a ‚Äútourist menu‚Äù with higher prices. I once got charged 20‚Ç¨ for _two donuts_.
* üö´ Don‚Äôt take pictures of people dressed-up as characters ‚Äî they will pressure you for money!
* üö´ [Restaurant] Spiked drinks. Keep a close eye on your drinks.
* üö´ [Airport] Someone asks if you need a taxi and takes you to a black car. (Use Uber/Lyft app instead.)
* üö´ [Airport] Someone holds a sign that says ‚ÄúUber‚Äù. (Use Uber/Lyft app instead.)
* üö´ [Airport] If you use Uber/Lyft, make sure Name, License Plate, and Car Model all match what the app says.
